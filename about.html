Our Story

Introduction

Join us as we rewrite the story of tea, one exquisite cup at a time.

Gitugi Tea Factory is located in Mumwe location, Othaya Division, Nyeri South District. It lies 37 degrees East and 2 degrees south of Equator and 22km from Nyeri town. The factory was built in 1983 and commissioned 1984. It serves 6050 growers.
Gitugi manufactures the following clones of tea:
31/8, 6/8 303/377
The factory manufactures Black CTC teas in the following grades:
Primary grades - BPI, PFI, PD, DI
Secondary grades - FI, DUST, TMF2

Tea Growing, Processing & Export

Choose Gitugi Tea Factory and discover the pinnacle of Kenyan tea

The processed tea is sold through Mombasa Auction, Direct Sales Over Seas, Direct Sales Local, Factory Door Sales and Kenya Tea Packers (KETEPA). Tea is transported to Mombasa for auction through rented trucks where it is received by KTDA personnel and stored in the KTDA Warehouses. The Marketing Department of KTDA allocates the tea to the appointed broker. The broker sends the samples and catalogues to the buyers. At the auction, tea is bought by the highest bidder. The green leaf received from the farmers is recorded and serves as the basis for payment to the farmers. Records of received green leaf are maintained for each grower. Once tea is processed, it is packed and invoiced showing the date, grade and quantities. At auction, the tea is sold to the highest bidder and thereafter the broker sends an auction report to the Factory.
