Gitugi Tea Factory Limited Company - Kenya is an award winning company in Kenya. Uncompromising Quality

We source our tea leaves from small-scale Kenyan farmers, renowned for their meticulous cultivation practices. Our stringent selection process ensures only the freshest, most vibrant leaves reach our processing facility.

State-of-the-Art Processing

We employ the latest tea processing technologies, meticulously crafted to preserve the natural characteristics of the leaves. From withering and rolling to fermentation and drying, each step is meticulously controlled to unlock the full potential of the tea.

Expertise and Dedication

Our team comprises experienced tea masters, passionate about the art of tea processing. Their dedication to meticulous craftsmanship and unwavering attention to detail shine through in every cup.

Sustainability Focus

We prioritize sustainable practices throughout our operations, from supporting our local farmers to minimizing our environmental impact. We are committed to ensuring that our tea production contributes positively to the environment and the communities we serve.

Diverse Tea Varieties

We offer a wide range of tea varieties, catering to every palate. From the bold and invigorating black teas to the delicate and nuanced green teas, we have a tea to suit every taste and occasion.

Ethical Sourcing

We believe in fair trade practices, ensuring our partnerships with tea growers are mutually beneficial and sustainable.

Innovation & Experimentation

We are constantly exploring new techniques and flavor combinations, pushing the boundaries of tea artistry to deliver truly unique and captivating experiences.

Transparency & Traceability

We provide clear information about our sourcing and production processes, allowing you to enjoy your tea with confidence.

Unique Varietals

We cultivate a diverse range of tea varieties, each offering a distinct character and flavor profile. Whether you prefer the robust notes of black tea, the delicate touch of green tea, or the floral essence of white tea, we have something to tantalize your taste buds.
